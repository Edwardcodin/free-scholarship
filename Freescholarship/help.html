-Master's degree:

A one to two-year degree that build upon a bachelor's degree and it is usually more specialized.

-Bachelor's degree:

A four year degree that cover a broad range of subjects and it is the most common type of degree in the United State of America.

-Doctoral degree:

A degree that requires several year of study and focuses on specific field of study.

-Associate'degree:

A two-year degree that focuses on a specific field or career path.
